Welcome to Motswedi Beauty Studio, located in Gaborone North, Botswana—a serene and growing suburb that blends tranquility with the vibrance of city life. At our studio, we celebrate the beauty and diversity of curly hair, specializing in protective styles like knotless braids, crochet hairstyles, and headband wigs. Our mission is to make every woman feel radiant and confident, offering expert craftsmanship and affordability. Nestled in this peaceful neighborhood, we provide a welcoming space where you can embrace your natural beauty and leave feeling empowered. Your crown, your way—right here in Gaborone North!
